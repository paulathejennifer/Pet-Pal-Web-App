FAQs

  • How do I add a pet to favorites? Click the heart icon on the pet card to add it to your favorites list.
  • How do I adopt a pet? Click the "Adopt" button on the pet card and fill out the adoption form on the next page.
  • Can I remove a pet from favorites? Yes, simply click the red heart icon again to remove a pet from your favorites page.
  • What is Quick View? Quick View shows more information about a pet without leaving the page.
  • Where can I see my favorite pets? Click "Favorites" in the navigation bar to see all your favorited pets.
